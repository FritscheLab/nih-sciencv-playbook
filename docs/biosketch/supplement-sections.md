# NIH Supplement sections (step-by-step)

## Personal Statement

- **3,500 characters**
- Tailor to your role on *this* project
- **No citations inside the text**
- Use referential language like “see Closely Related Product #1”

## Contributions to Science

- Up to **5 contributions**
- **2,000 characters each**
- **No citations inside the text**
- Each contribution should map to one or more of your **Other Significant Products**

## Honors

- Up to **15** honors/awards
- Prioritize meaningful external recognitions
